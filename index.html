Eddie Romeo's Autos

We are devoted Alfa Romeo enthusiasts based in Lithuania, passionately committed to owning, driving, restoring, and selling these iconic cars. What began as helping friends and relatives find their dream Alfa Romeos has evolved into a small business focused on turning our customers' Alfa Romeo aspirations into reality. Utilizing our extensive knowledge and experience, we specialize in selling high-quality Alfa Romeo cars both in Lithuania and internationally.

On this page Eddie Romeo's Autos, you'll discover some of the finest Alfa Romeo vehicles currently and previously available for sale through us.

In 2023, we proudly expanded our services to embrace the future of automotive innovation by incorporating electric cars alongside our long-established expertise in Alfa Romeo brand. This exciting transition not only reflects our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility but also positions us as a comprehensive destination for all your automotive needs. Together, we’re driving towards a greener tomorrow. Check the current Electric cars for sale here: Eddie Romeo's Autos Electric
